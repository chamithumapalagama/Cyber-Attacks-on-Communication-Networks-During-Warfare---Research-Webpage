Cyber capabilities are now a core pillar of national security. Governments and militaries use them to: Detect and prevent attacks in real-time Disrupt enemy communications or infrastructure without deploying physical forces Secure critical systems, such as air defense networks, military satellites, and logistics operations For example, the U.S. Cyber Command (USCYBERCOM) was established to conduct both defensive and offensive cyber operations, integrating cyber with traditional military strategy.
